About Little Voices

Dedicated to helping children communicate confidently and effectively through personalized speech and language therapy

Creating Confident Communicators

At Little Voices, we specialize in providing comprehensive speech and language therapy services for children of all ages. Our approach is centered around creating a fun, engaging, and supportive environment where children can develop their communication skills at their own pace.

With over 10 years of experience, our certified speech-language pathologists are dedicated to helping each child reach their full potential through evidence-based practices tailored to individual needs.

  • Certified Speech-Language Pathologists
  • Evidence-Based Therapy Approaches
  • Personalized Treatment Plans
  • Family-Centered Care

Who We Help

Our specialized services support children with various communication challenges

Late Talkers

Children who are developing vocabulary and language skills later than their peers

Articulation Disorders

Children who have difficulty producing speech sounds correctly

Language Disorders

Children who struggle with understanding or using language effectively

Autism Spectrum

Children on the autism spectrum who need support with social communication

Stuttering

Children who experience disruptions in the flow of their speech

Reading Difficulties

Children who struggle with reading and literacy skills
